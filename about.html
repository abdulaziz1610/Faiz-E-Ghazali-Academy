About Faiz E Ghazali Academy

Dedicated to spreading the light of Islamic knowledge to learners worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide authentic Islamic education to children, adults, and families globally through online learning, focusing on Quran, Hadith, and Islamic teachings.

Our Vision

To inspire a global community of Muslims to live by Islamic principles and foster a deep connection with Allah (SWT) through knowledge and practice.

Our Journey

Founded in 2024, Faiz E Ghazali Academy began with a mission to make quality Islamic education accessible to everyone. Today, we serve students worldwide, offering courses in Quranic recitation, Tajweed, memorization, and other Islamic sciences.

Our Core Values

  • Authenticity: Providing accurate and reliable Islamic teachings.
  • Accessibility: Making Islamic education available to learners of all ages and backgrounds.
  • Excellence: Maintaining high standards of teaching and learning.
  • Community: Building a supportive and inclusive global Islamic community.
